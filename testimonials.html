“We heard about HomeIntel from a mailer we received and thought we would give it a try. Once we signed up, they sent us a helpful email immediately identifying some of the things they thought were worth investigating for energy use reduction. They also identified how much energy was being spent by different items in our house that we had no idea were so costly. The Energy Coach Chris came out to our house and the first thing he did was to suggest that we reduce the pool pump's hours. There are always competing concerns: the pool service had set the pump to run a lot to prevent algae from forming in the pool, but that number of hours was expensive. We were able to find a happy medium between the two options. The coach recommended resetting the circulation pump - previously running at 1000 watts/hour for 8 hours - to 3 hours and reducing the sweep hours as well. He also moved the timeframe when the pumps ran so we would be set for lower electric rates when the Time Of Use rate plan from PG&E kicks in. In addition, we have Marantz audio equipment in our media room which used 85 watts in standby mode and 850 watts when operating. We now have them plugged into a power strip and we simply flip the switch on when we want music. All in all, it has made a significant difference in our monthly bills.”

Tom M.
Atherton
